Executive summary

A strong month for efficiency

March delivered Northwind’s best return on ad spend of the quarter. Total spend held flat at $48,250 while blended ROAS climbed to 4.1x, lifting attributed revenue to $197,800. Brand Search and Meta prospecting carried the period.

Conversions dipped 2.3% against February, driven by softening Instagram retargeting CTR. We recommend reallocating a portion of catalog spend toward the highest-efficiency search campaigns next month.
