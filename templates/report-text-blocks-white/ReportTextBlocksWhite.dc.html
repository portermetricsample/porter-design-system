Overview

Spend held flat while revenue climbed 14%

The efficiency story of Q1: we kept budget steady across channels and let creative testing do the heavy lifting. ROAS rose from 3.5x to 4.1x.

What drove the change

Three shifts compounded over the quarter. First, we consolidated spend into the two channels with the strongest incremental return and paused four underperforming campaigns by week three.

Second, the creative refresh launched in February outperformed the prior library on every format, with video carrying the largest lift. Third, a tighter audience strategy lowered CPA without sacrificing volume.
