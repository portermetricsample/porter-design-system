Mono, uppercase, tracked label that sits directly above a headline. Names the section/topic in 1–4 words; the headline carries the message.

```tsx
<Eyebrow>Marketing reports</Eyebrow>
<Heading level="h2">Your data, <strong>on brand</strong></Heading>
```

No props. Defaults to Porter purple; override `style={{ color }}` on dark surfaces (e.g. yellow on the signature dark).
