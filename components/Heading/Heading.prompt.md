Porter display headline — massive Inter, tight tracking. The protagonist of every layout. Wrap one key word in `<strong>` to tint it purple.

```tsx
<Heading level="display">Report<br/><strong>faster</strong></Heading>
<Heading level="h2">Your data, <strong>on brand</strong></Heading>
```

Levels: `display` (covers, uppercase, 72–140px) · `h1` (48–88px) · `h2` (32–56px). Follow the two-line stacked pattern: a normal word on top, the accented key word huge below.
